Behind the Fire

My Creative Process

Every commission is a tailored journey. Here's how I bring your ideas to life on natural wood.

Depending on the size and complexity, a standard commission usually takes 10 to 21 business days. Portraits especially require multiple layers of burning and shading for that premium, handcrafted look.

Pyrography is permanent as it physically carbonizes the wood fibers. However, like any natural wood product, prolonged exposure to direct sunlight can cause the wood itself to lighten or darken. I apply high-end UV-resistant sealants to every piece to ensure your investment lasts for generations.

High-resolution photos with clear lighting work best for premium pyrography. I manually review every photo submitted and provide professional recommendations on how to achieve the best wood-burned representation.

Taking Care of Your Art

Your pyrography piece is a natural, living product and should be treated with the respect an original creation deserves.

Avoid Humidity

Keep the piece in a dry, stable environment to prevent any natural wood warping.

Dusting Only

Clean exclusively with a soft, dry micro-fiber cloth. Avoid all harsh chemicals.
