My Story & Passion

I didn't choose pyrography; pyrography chose me. What started as a hobby in my small home workshop has grown into a lifelong dedication to the art of wood burning.

With over a decade of experience, I've learned that every wood grain is unique and requires a different touch. From the aromatic scent of burning cedar to the smooth finish of maple, I find beauty in the natural imperfections of the wood.

My Approach

Artistic Philosophy

Sustainability

I use ethically sourced, reclaimed, or naturally fallen wood for all my artwork.

Precision

Every single line is intentional, burned with extreme care and microscopic detail.

Passion

I treat every commission as if it were for my own home, pouring soul into every burn.

My Creative Sanctuary

My studio is where time slows down. Surrounded by various pyrography tools, sketches, and hundreds of wood slices, I find my focus. It's here that ideas transform into permanent pieces of art.

02.
Natural Oils

The Ethical Choice

Philosophy of Wood & Fire

Every commission begins with respect for the natural resource. I am committed to an eco-conscious creative process that honors the heritage of the wood.

Ethical Sourcing

I work exclusively with local wood suppliers who provide reclaimed or sustainably harvested timber. No fresh-cut trees are used.

Traditional Methods

By choosing hand-burning over industrial lasers, I ensure that each piece has a unique soul and a carbon-conscious footprint.

100% Sustainable

From reclaimed wood to organic mineral oil finishes.
