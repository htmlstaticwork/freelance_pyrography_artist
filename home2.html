Born From Ash & Embers

Pyrography is more than a technique; it's a dialogue between the artist and the soul of the wood. My journey began with a simple soldering iron and a piece of scrap cedar.

Explore My Work My Story
Inspiration

Passion & Purpose

Nature is my greatest muse. The way light filters through trees, the fierce gaze of a hawk—these are the stories I burn into wood. Each piece is a meditation, taking anywhere from 20 to 100 hours of focused craftsmanship.

"The wood dictates the art. My job is simply to listen and bring it to life."
