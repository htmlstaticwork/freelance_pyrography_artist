Preservation

Heritage Care Guide

Wood is a living material. To ensure your custom burn remains a family heirloom for generations, follow these simple care steps.

UV Protection

Burning is permanent, but direct sunlight can fade wood tones over decades. Display your art in a well-lit room, but away from direct window glare.

Humidity Health

Extreme temperature or moisture changes can cause wood to expand or contract. Humidity levels between 40-50% are ideal for natural wood slabs.

Soft Cleaning

Never use chemical cleaners or wet wipes. A simple pass with a dry, soft microfiber cloth is all that's required to clear dust from the grain.
