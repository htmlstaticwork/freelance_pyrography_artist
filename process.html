Behind the Fire

My Creative Process

Every commission is a tailored journey. Here's how I bring your ideas to life on natural wood.

01

Discovery & Design

It starts with a conversation. We discuss your vision, the level of detail, and the type of wood. I create a digital mockup or a detailed hand-sketch for your approval before any burning begins.

02

Surface Preparation

The wood is hand-selected and sanded to a microscopic smoothness. A fine surface is crucial for achieving consistent burns and professional-level detail.

03

The Main Burn

Using professional wood-burning pens, I burn the outlines and layers of shading. This phase is slow and permanent, requiring hours of concentration to achieve photo-realistic depth.
